What are eSIMs?

An eSIM is an embedded SIM, offering the same features as the physical SIM minus the hardware. In other words, the physical SIM is a card one inserts into their smartphone. It stores various information, such as one's phone number, text messages, contact numbers, location, and data about the mobile's network. Additionally, SIM cards provide mobile data enabling internet connection on smartphones.
